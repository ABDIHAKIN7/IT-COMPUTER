How to Navigate

Use the navigation bar at the top of every page to switch between Home, Gallery, Booking, Help, and About pages.

Using the Gallery

In the Gallery page, you can view three images and a data table. Hover over images to see alt text.

Booking a Guest

On the Booking page, fill out all required fields (name, major, gender, hobbies) and click Submit to send your info.

Table Instructions

Our table includes student names, majors, and countries. Table headers are highlighted and the footer provides a summary.
